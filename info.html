What is BMI?

BMI is also known as Body Mass Index. Doctors use BMI as an indicator of whether the person is overweight, normal, or underweight. It also determines the persons overall health risks and whether that person should loose weight.

As you can see, a range of 18.5 and below would be classified as underweight. An ideal person would have their BMI range somewhere from 18.5 to 24.9. An Overweight person would have their BMI go from 25 to 29.9, while a BMI of 30 and over is considered obese. Its important to have a normal BMI in order to avoid health problems.       2. While junk food may taste great, it is one of the prime causes for overweight or obesity. Examples include fries, burgers, and oreos. Try to eliminate some junk food from your normal diet.

  2. Exercise
      3. When your body exercises, it burns off calories. If you can burn off more calories than you consume, then you are loosing weight.
